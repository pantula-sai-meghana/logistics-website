Welcome to SEA Transport, your reliable partner for all your transportation needs. Sea freight is a shipping method used by businesses to transport large amounts of goods overseas by sea using cargo ships. Since it’s cost-effective, most businesses prefer to use this mode of transportation, when they have to move a huge quantity of goods. Sea freight is distinct from traditional modes of goods transportation as ideally large loads of good, or heavy machinery is shipped through Sea freight. In Sea freight, goods are transported in large metal containers (You must’ve probably seen one if you have been to a seaport). There are fewer restrictions imposed on sea freight. Sea freight is also used to transport several crucial commodities for a country, like Oil, Defence Equipment, and Aircraft.

Features of Sea Transport

Cargo Capacity

Sea transport has a large cargo capacity, making it ideal for transporting bulky goods over long distances.

Cost-Effective

It is one of the most cost-effective methods of transport, especially for international trade.

Environmental Impact

Sea transport has a lower environmental impact compared to air transport in terms of carbon emissions per ton-km.

Advantages of Sea Transport

Cost-Effective

Sea transport is generally cheaper compared to air and land transport, especially for bulky goods.

Large Capacity

Ships can carry large volumes of goods, making it ideal for transporting heavy and bulky items.

Safety

Sea transport is considered one of the safest modes of transport, with lower accident rates.

Environmental Impact

Sea transport has a lower carbon footprint per ton of cargo compared to other modes of transport.

Global Reach

Ships can travel to almost any part of the world, providing extensive global coverage.

Versatility

Sea transport can handle a wide variety of goods, from liquids to solids and everything in between.
